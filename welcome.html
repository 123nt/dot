Welcome to Dot

Important Note

This is a privacy-focused messaging app designed to protect your communications.

  • Your information will never be leaked or sold to any third parties
  • Your username is your unique identity - keep it safe
  • Connect with others using their usernames
  • Usernames must be appropriate and readable
  • Offensive or inappropriate usernames will be rejected

We prioritize your privacy and security. No contact information is required - just your username and password.

Message Privacy

Important Note

Your privacy is our top priority. Here's how we protect your messages:

  • Messages auto-delete after 8 hours
  • 8-hour timer is non-adjustable for maximum privacy
  • Messages are permanently deleted from both sides
  • Your username is your unique ID
  • Add usernames carefully - they work like phone numbers

Once messages are deleted, they're gone forever - from your chat, the recipient's chat, and our servers. Be mindful of what you share.
